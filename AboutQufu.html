About Qufu

Qufu, a famous tourist city in China, is 80 kilometers south of Mount Tai. It is the hometown of Confucius, a great thinker, educator, philosopher, and the founder of Confucianism; it has three Historic Sites: The Confucius Temple, the Confucius Mansion, and the Confucius Forest; it enjoys a worldwide fame as a traditional Chinese culture site; and it is known as the "Eastern Holy City".
